Game Sound FX Pad Privacy Policy


Game Sound FX Pad("The App") may or may not collect personal data using the following services.


Read and Write Storage

"The App" will save playing audio to your device when
Record button was tapped and stops when Record Stop button was tapped from screen.
"The App" will save project file on your device.
Some sample sound files are saved on your device at first time.
"The App" reads sound file from your device.
"The App" reads project file from your device.


Full Network Access

"The App" shows ads which
AdMob Google Inc advertising service("AdMob") provides.
"AdMob" collects Cookies and Usage data using the internet connection.
"AdMob" Privacy Policy

Google Play Store
"The App" shows video ads which
Unity Technologies developer service("Unity-ads") provides.
"Unity-ads" Privacy Policy

Amazon Appstore
"The App" shows ads which
Amazon Mobile Ad Network provides.
Amazon Mobile Ad Network Privacy Policy

"The App" needs to open browser to see this page.
