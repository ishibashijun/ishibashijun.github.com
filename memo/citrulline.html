Citrulline

  • Citrulline increases athletic performance in high-intesity anaerobic exercises. [1][2][4][5]
  • Citrulline relieves post exercise muscle soreness. [1]
  • Citrulline does not provide acute ergogenic benefits in high-intensity cycle sprint. [3]
