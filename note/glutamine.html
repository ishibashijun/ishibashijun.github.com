Glutamine

  • Glutamine may stimulate the growth of some tumors. [1]
  • Glutamine enriched oral diets do not stimulate tumor growth. [2]
  • Faster muscle strength recovery and diminished muscle soreness. [3]
  • Doses of up to 0.65/kg body mass did not result in abnormal plasma ammonia levels. [4]
