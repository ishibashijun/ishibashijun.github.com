Effect screen(2)



  • ON button: to turn the effect on.
  • Interval knob: interval.
  • Wet knob: 0 is 100% DRY and 1 is 100% WET.
  • Time knob: delay time in second.
  • Feedback knob: feedback.
  • Room size knob: room size.
  • Dry knob: dry level.
  • Wet knob: wet level.
