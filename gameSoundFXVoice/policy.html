Game Sound FX Voice Privacy Policy


Game Sound FX Voice("The App") may or may not collect personal data using the following services.


Recording Audio

"The App" records audio from mic when
Record Voice button was tapped and stops when Stop button was tapped from top screen.
If the recorded audio was not saved, it will be disposed and if
Record Sound FX button was tapped then after desireable seconds Stop Recording button was tapped or after about 12 seconds("recording procedure")
it will be saved to your device storage.


Read and Write Storage

"The app" will save recorded audio to your device if "recording procedure" was taken.
"The app" does not read any file from your device.


Full Network Access

"The app" shows ads which
AdMob Google Inc advertising service("AdMob") provides.
"AdMob" collects Cookies and Usage data using the internet connection.
